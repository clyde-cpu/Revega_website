Humble Beginnings

The popular Revega Specialty Coffee and Pizza Café, located at Unitop Mall in Dasmarinas, Cavite,tells the story of a devoted couple,  Velus Dolor Garcia, an Engineer with a multinational semiconductor background, and Regielyn Co. Garcia, a passionate homemaker and experienced food industry professional. Together, they own and manage this beloved establishment, combining their skills and love for pastries and cooking to create a haven for quality coffee and delectable pizza.

The name 'Revega' beautifully encapsulates their partnership and their shared dream of delivering excellence in the world of coffee and pizza."

About us

Revega Specialty Coffee and Pizza, the newest coffee shop in Cavite, is locally owned and locally focused. It offers handcrafted beverages and food every day from Dasmariñas City. Featuring a drive-thru and friendly service, Revega Cafe is the perfect space for coffee, meet-ups, and co-working.
